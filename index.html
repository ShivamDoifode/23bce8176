PROJECT OVERVIEW

Smart Street Lighting System

Abstract

The Smart Street Light System is an automated framework designed to optimize road lighting and reduce power consumption by activating streetlights only when vehicles or pedestrians are detected. This system employs motion sensors to identify movement, and uses Arduino to control LED streetlights, which automatically adjust their brightness based on detected activity. By turning lights on only when necessary and off when no movement is detected, the system significantly conserves energy. Additionally, the integration of ambient light sensors allows the system to adjust brightness according to the time of day or weather conditions, further enhancing energy efficiency. This innovative approach to street lighting not only simplifies and automates the process but also contributes to substantial energy savings and environmental conservation.

Components

  • 1 Arduino (microcontroller)
  • 2 PIR Sensor (detects pedestrians)
  • 3 Ultrasonic Sensor (detects vehicles)
  • 4 LDR (detects ambient light)
  • 5 LEDs
  • 6 5V/12V Relay modules (to control high-power lights)
  • 7 Power Supply (9V/12V)
  • 8 Jumper wires, resistors, transistors, breadboards
  • 9 Waterproof casings, mounting frames
